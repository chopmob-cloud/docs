Recurr is AlgoVoi's recurring-payment layer. Every cycle, a fresh invoice is generated and emailed to the customer as a standard payment link. The customer pays from their own wallet, on their own schedule. AlgoVoi never holds keys or standing payment authority.

## How it differs from direct debit

| | Traditional direct debit | Recurr |
|---|---|---|
| Who holds payment authority | Bank / PSP | Nobody â€” customer pays each invoice |
| Customer action per cycle | None (autopulled) | Click link, pay from wallet |
| Missed payment | Silent retry | Dunning emails, then subscription paused |
| Revocation | Contact merchant or bank | Customer clicks cancel link, instant |
| AlgoVoi custody of funds | No | No |

## Core model

**Customers** â€” a wallet address associated with a tenant's merchant account. Identified by wallet, not email.

**Subscriptions** â€” the billing schedule: amount, currency, chain, cadence, and a cancel secret. Cadence options are `daily`, `weekly`, `monthly`, `quarterly`, `annual`, or `custom` (fixed interval in days).

**Invoices** â€” generated automatically at each cycle. Each invoice is a standard AlgoVoi payment link. When the customer pays on-chain, the invoice is marked paid and the next cycle is scheduled.

## Supported chains

All seven AlgoVoi networks: Algorand, VOI, Hedera, Stellar, Base, Solana, Tempo. Chain is set per subscription.

## Creating a customer

```bash
curl -X POST https://api.algovoi.co.uk/v1/customers \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $ALGOVOI_API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "wallet_address": "CUSTOMER_WALLET",
    "email": "customer@example.com"
  }'
```

## Creating a subscription

```bash
curl -X POST https://api.algovoi.co.uk/v1/subscriptions \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $ALGOVOI_API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "customer_id": "uuid",
    "amount_minor": 1000000,
    "currency": "USDC",
    "chain": "algorand_mainnet",
    "cadence": "monthly",
    "notify_lead_hours": 48
  }'
```

`amount_minor` is in microunits (1,000,000 = 1.00 USDC). `notify_lead_hours` controls how early the invoice-ready email is sent before the due date.

## Customer self-cancel

Every subscription has a `cancel_url` â€” a single-use link sent to the customer. Clicking it shows a confirmation page; submitting cancels immediately and rotates the secret so the link cannot be replayed.

The cancel link is included in every invoice email. No login, no account, no friction.

## Customer portal

`recurr.algovoi.co.uk` is a wallet-connect portal where customers can view all their active subscriptions across every merchant who has billed that wallet, and cancel any of them. Supported wallets:

- **EVM** (Base, Tempo): MetaMask
- **Solana**: Phantom
- **Algorand / VOI**: Pera, Defly, Lute
- **Stellar**: Freighter, Albedo, xBull, LOBSTR
- **Hedera**: HashPack, Blade, Kabila (via WalletConnect)

## Hosted authorisation page (Tier 2)

For Tier 2 standing authorities â€” where a pull executor draws on-chain on behalf of the customer â€” a one-time wallet signature is collected before the authority activates. The hosted page at `recurr.algovoi.co.uk/{signing_token}` guides the customer through:

1. Resolve the token (validates it is unexpired and merchant context loads)
2. Review the authority summary: merchant, amount cap, cadence, chain, expiry
3. Connect wallet and sign the authority transaction
4. Confirm â€” the backend activates the authority and notifies the merchant

The signing token is single-use, has a 24-hour TTL, and is never logged. The page applies sanctions screening at confirm time (SAMLA 2018 s.20 compliant).

## Dunning

When an invoice is not paid within the cycle window, AlgoVoi's dunning worker sends follow-up emails on an exponential backoff schedule (1 day, 3 days, 7 days). After the configured maximum attempts, the subscription moves to `cancelled`.

## Webhooks

Recurr fires the standard [outbound webhook](/integrations/outbound-webhooks) events:

| Event | Fired when |
|---|---|
| `subscription.created` | New subscription created |
| `subscription.invoice.paid` | Customer pays an invoice |
| `subscription.cancelled` | Subscription cancelled (any reason) |
| `subscription.paused` | Subscription paused |
| `subscription.resumed` | Subscription resumed |

## Dashboard

Subscriptions and customers are visible in the tenant dashboard under **Recurr**. Operators can create, pause, resume, and cancel subscriptions, and view invoice history per subscription.
